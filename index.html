Hi, I'm Erin. I'm a third year Software Engineering and Society student at McMaster University. My hobbies consist or reading, running, and sometimes coding. My favourite types of books are fantasy and science fiction but I like to read a variety of everything. My current favourite author is Brandon Sanderson and my all time favourite series is Mistborn. I like to track all my reading through Goodreads. In addition to reading I’ve been a fan of running distance for a long time. I did track throughout school and I like to try and keep myself active in the warmer months. I’m not very competitive however so I don’t do races unless they have fun themes like zombie run.
Now for the hobby that you probably care about, coding. I like to create things at Hackathons, generally with sillier themes behind them. I prefer to keep my more useful coding applications for school work because it’s a lot easier to motivate myself to create something that makes me laugh. I still have however made some pretty cool things for fun as seen in my portfolio section. This year I took on a coding challenge where I code every single day in 2017. You can follow my Github to see what I focused on creating and learning. The goal with this project was to motivate me to learn how to program better through practice without it feeling unachievable. The creation of this website is part of the project. I’d say so far it’s a success and I can’t want to see what I’ve made by the end of the year. I’ve even been reflecting on what I’ve learned in the repo as well because personal growth is pretty important to me. I’m hoping that I can be pretty strong in web development by the end of the project and I’m on track to meet that.
